About

Since the dawn of Hip-Hop, crate digging has been an essential element in the creation of beats. Sampling has evolved into a unique craft for beatmakers worldwide. At Crate Monkey, we offer a digital solution for discovering obscure and unique samples for your records.

Unlike traditional sample libraries, Crate Monkey takes a unique approach. Instead of simply presenting you with pre-selected tracks, we provide you with a randomly generated album. It's your task to explore each album and go through the tracks to find the perfect sample that fuels your creativity.

How to Use

  • 1. Choose a Genre in the dropdown menu.
  • 2. Choose a style based on the genre you selected and hit search!
  • 3. Crate Monkey will generate the Title, Year released and album cover based on the parameters chosen .
  • 4. Listen to the album and find samples!
